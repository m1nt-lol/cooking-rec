Cooking Recipes

  • Ingredients:

    • 200g spaghetti
    • 100g pancetta
    • 2 large eggs
    • 50g Parmesan cheese
    • Salt and pepper

    Instructions:

    1. Cook spaghetti according to package directions. Drain, reserving a cup of pasta water.

    2. In a pan, cook pancetta until crispy.

    3. Beat eggs and mix with Parmesan cheese. Add cooked pasta and pancetta to the egg mixture and toss quickly.

    4. Add pasta water to achieve a creamy texture. Season with salt and pepper. Serve hot.

  • Ingredients:

    • 1 cup broccoli florets
    • 1 bell pepper, sliced
    • 1 carrot, sliced
    • 2 tbsp soy sauce
    • 1 tbsp sesame oil

    Instructions:

    1. Heat sesame oil in a wok over medium heat. Add vegetables and stir-fry for 5-7 minutes until tender-crisp.

    2. Add soy sauce and toss to coat the vegetables.

    3. Serve hot with rice or noodles.
